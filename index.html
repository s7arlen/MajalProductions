Pioneering smokeless cooking solutions from the heart of Bantwal, Karnataka for over a decade

Our Story

Even while most people now cook using modern stoves and ovens, many still rely on traditional mud stoves — especially in coastal Karnataka, where they're used for cooking rice and sanitary purposes.

The original mud stove required enormous time and firewood. With rapidly rising fuel costs, we developed the innovative 3-in-1 Astra Ole, which serves both cooking and water heating with just a single fire.

Built in Bantwal, Dakshina Kannada (Mangaluru), our smokeless burner has been trusted by over 3,000 families and continues to grow every day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services

What makes the Astra Ole different from traditional mud stoves?▼

The Majal 3-in-1 Astra Ole is a revolutionary smokeless burner that combines cooking and water heating in a single fire. Unlike traditional mud stoves that produce excessive smoke and waste fuel, our Astra Ole is 100% smokeless, fuel-efficient, and can use agricultural waste as firewood. It provides a clean, healthy kitchen environment while being economical.

How long do your products last?▼

All our products are engineered for durability and are guaranteed to last 20+ years with proper maintenance. Our stainless steel boilers are rust-free and have been tested for quality and leakage. We stand behind the longevity of our products with comprehensive warranties.

Is installation difficult?▼

No, installation is straightforward and hassle-free. Our team provides professional installation guidance and support. We ensure that your product is installed correctly and you understand all its features and maintenance requirements. Most installations are completed within a day.

What capacity options are available for boilers?▼

Our stainless steel boilers come in multiple capacity options to suit different family sizes and requirements. From compact sizes for small households to larger capacities for commercial use, we have options available. Please contact us to discuss the best capacity for your needs.

Can I use the Astra Ole for other purposes besides cooking?▼

Yes! The 3-in-1 Astra Ole is perfect for cooking rice, preparing other traditional dishes, and heating water for bathing. The single fire serves multiple purposes, making it incredibly versatile for households that follow traditional cooking practices.

What is your warranty and after-sales service?▼

We provide comprehensive warranty coverage on all our products. Our dedicated after-sales service team is always ready to help with maintenance, repairs, and any questions you may have. We stand by our commitment to customer satisfaction and quality service.
